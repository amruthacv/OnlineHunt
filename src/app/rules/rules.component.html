    What is Quest ?
  • Quest is an online puzzle. It consists of multiple levels and at each level you’d be provided with a question. Solve the question one by one and move on. :)
  • The hunt will be a weeklong event. End date will be announced later.
  • Answers can be made up letters, numbers or a combination of both.
  • To make things a bit easier and to reduce the topics under sun, answers will revolve around qb-universe and technology.
  • Answers are not Case-Sensitive nor space-Sensitive. Also answer does not contain special characters like % , - , . etc. For example, if your answer is Luke Skywalker, you can answer in any case you want. like 'Luke Skywalker' or 'LUKE SKYWALKER’ or ‘LuKe SkYwAlkEr’ or 'luke skywalker'.
  • For discussion and clues related to Quest, join Quest G+ community.
  • Frequency of clues will depend on the difficulty of each level.
  • The Leaderboard shows top 20 Players current position and last level completion time.
  • Ranking of Players in same level are time dependant i.e. player who completes the level first stays first.
  • Players who cracks the first prize question[TBA] first will awarded first. Second and third prize questions will be announced after that.
  • A player can only win one prize.
  • Play fair and let everyone else play. Refrain from DDoS/hacking. ;)
